<Aside type="note" title="About chain selectors">
  A **chain selector** is Chainlink's unique numeric identifier for each blockchain network. Unlike chain IDs, chain
  selectors are globally unique across all supported networks. CRE Connect uses chain selectors — not chain IDs — to
  identify the chain a [Watcher](/crec/concepts/watchers), [Smart Account](/crec/concepts/smart-accounts), or
  [Operation](/crec/concepts/operations) is bound to.
</Aside>
