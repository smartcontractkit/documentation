## Guides

### [Using CCIP Local Simulator in RemixIDE](/chainlink-local/build/ccip/remix/local-simulator)

This guide helps you to test [Chainlink CCIP getting started guide](/ccip/getting-started) locally in RemixIDE. You will deploy a CCIP sender contract and a CCIP receiver contract, and interact with the local simulator to send data from the sender contract to the receiver contract.
