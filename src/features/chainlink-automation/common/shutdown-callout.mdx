<Aside type="caution" title="Chainlink Automation Sunset Notice">
Chainlink Automation v1.x sunsets on June 30, 2026.
Chainlink Automation v2.1 sunsets on July 31, 2026 (testnet: June 24, 2026).

Migrate to the [Chainlink Runtime Environment (CRE)](/cre), which provides all Automation functionality and more. Migrate your existing upkeeps before these dates to avoid service disruption.

**Reduced Service Windows**

To help users identify Automation jobs that are still active and prepare for the Automation sunset, we will temporarily reduce the Automation service during the following windows:

- July 15, 9:00 AM–1:00 PM EST (4 hours)
- July 22, 9:00 AM–5:00 PM EST (8 hours)
- July 27, 9:00 AM–July 28, 9:00 AM EST (24 hours)

During these windows, existing upkeeps may not execute as expected. These temporary service reductions are intended to help identify workloads that have not yet been migrated to CRE before the final sunset dates.

To ensure uninterrupted operation, migrate your upkeeps to CRE before the applicable sunset date.

See the [Automation to CRE migration guide](/cre/reference/cla-migration) for a full concept mapping and code examples.

</Aside>
